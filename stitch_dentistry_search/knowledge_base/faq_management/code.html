What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans to make dental care accessible. We are in-network with providers such as Delta Dental, MetLife, Cigna, Aetna, and many more. Please call our office for specific plan details.

How do I reschedule an appointment?

You can easily reschedule your appointment directly through our mobile app. Navigate to the 'My Appointments' section, select the appointment you wish to change, and follow the prompts to find a new time.

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays. For dental emergencies outside of these hours, please call our emergency line.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans through third-party financing partners like CareCredit. This allows you to break down the cost of your treatment into manageable monthly payments. Please ask our front desk for more information.
